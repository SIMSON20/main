Mission

StudentRentit.com is committed to providing the best off-campus housing solution for students, apartment communities and private owners.

Vision

To become the most adaptable, user-friendly and intuitive student housing resource.

StudentRentIt.com is the most student oriented internet listing site in the world. We are determined to create a tool that gives students a listing service that actually works, has accurate information and allows them to do everything that involves student housing in one portal.

Students can finally connect directly with apartment communities, private owners and share information with their parents. In addition, they can find roommates, post subleases, and browse private listings!

We guarantee that we will continue to develop tools that will bring a sense of ease in locating, signing and living in off-campus student housing across the United States.

Company History

StudentRentIt.com was created by two Realtor’s in San Marcos, Texas. After working with students and helping them find apartments, they noticed that there isn’t a solution that makes finding off-campus housing easy. Other websites have SPAM or only a handful of apartments on the website. Nowhere else can they connect with college students exclusively, find roommates, rate apartments, and locate those hard to find homes and private listings.

StudentRentIt.com launched their Beta version in May of 2013 in the San Marcos area exclusively for Texas State University Students.

StuentRentIt.com launched its revamped website in January 2014 and expanded into College Station, TX. The founders plan to expand to nearby Texas markets and nationally.
